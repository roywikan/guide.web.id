What do foreigners first do when they first land in Indonesia?

First Time in Indonesia The Guide — June 8, 2023 · 0 Comment

When foreigners first land in Indonesia, they typically engage in various activities and actions. Here are What do foreigners first do when they first land in Indonesia :

  1. Clear immigration and customs.
  2. Exchange currency at the airport.
  3. Arrange transportation to their accommodation.
  4. Check-in at their hotel or guesthouse.
  5. Familiarize themselves with local transportation options.
  6. Sample Indonesian cuisine at a local restaurant.
  7. Explore the local neighborhood or city.
  8. Visit a tourist information center.
  9. Take a taxi or ride-sharing service to their destination.
  10. Try to learn a few basic Indonesian phrases.
  11. Connect to local mobile network or Wi-Fi.
  12. Visit a local market to buy essentials or souvenirs.
  13. Acquaint themselves with the local customs and etiquette.
  14. Take a walk around the area to get oriented.
  15. Rest and recover from the journey.
  16. Capture photos of their surroundings.
  17. Purchase a local SIM card for their phone.
  18. Seek out local attractions or landmarks.
  19. Sample traditional Indonesian snacks or street food.
  20. Seek assistance or guidance from hotel staff.
  21. Engage with locals and learn about their culture.
  22. Locate nearby amenities like pharmacies or convenience stores.
  23. Take note of emergency contact numbers.
  24. Find a reliable source of drinking water.
  25. Admire the natural beauty of the surroundings.
  26. Seek out a local tour or activity to participate in.
  27. Arrange for transportation to their next destination.
  28. Visit a local café or coffee shop.
  29. Find a reliable map or navigation app for getting around.
  30. Embrace the unique sights, sounds, and smells of Indonesia.
